Content Design & UX Writing

How Advizmo communicates with users

01 Brand Voice

Every word inside the product should build trust, reduce financial anxiety and increase clarity.

Voice Characteristics

🧘
Calm
Measured confidence, never rushed or urgent
💪
Confident
Clear facts without hedging
👔
Professional
Business-appropriate, respectful
🔍
Transparent
Clear what's happening and why
🤝
Human
Natural language, not robotic
🎯
Helpful
Anticipates questions, guides gently

Examples

✓ Good
"Your portfolio grew 5.23% this month."
✕ Bad
"Congrats!! Your portfolio is absolutely crushing it! 🚀"
✓ Good
"You have $2,340 available to invest."
✕ Bad
"You should really invest more money right now!"
✓ Good
"This recommendation is based on your risk tolerance."
✕ Bad
"We guarantee 12% returns annually."

02 Writing Principles

Every sentence should reduce uncertainty, reduce cognitive load, and increase confidence.

Plain Language

✓ Use
"Available cash"
✕ Avoid
"Liquid assets"

Short Sentences

✓ Use
"Your portfolio is up 3.2% this month. That's $4,800 in growth."
✕ Avoid
"Your portfolio has experienced a 3.2% increase this month, which represents approximately $4,800 in growth across your diversified holdings."

Active Voice

✓ Use
"We transferred $500 to your savings."
✕ Avoid
"$500 was transferred to your savings."

08 Empty States

Every empty state should include title, explanation, and next action. Never blame the user.

📊

Your dashboard is ready

Connect your first account to see your financial overview.

🎯

Set your first goal

Goals help you save for what matters most.

⚙️

Automate your finances

Set up automatic investments and bill payments.

09 Errors

Every error must explain what happened, why, and how to fix it.

Insufficient funds

Your checking account has $1,234.56 available. The requested transfer of $5,000.00 exceeds your balance.

Connection failed

We couldn't connect to your bank. Please check your internet connection and try again.

Transfer failed

Your transfer couldn't be completed. This usually happens when the account information is incorrect.

10 Success Messages

Keep messages concise. Be specific. Don't overcelebrate.

Investment complete

$500 invested in VTI.

Transfer complete

$1,000 transferred to Savings.

Bill paid

Electric bill of $145 paid.

Account connected

Chase account connected.

12 AI Conversations

The AI should sound knowledgeable, calm, and helpful. Never pressure, guarantee, or predict certainty.

✨ Advizmo AI

Based on your risk tolerance and goals, I recommend investing $500 in a diversified portfolio. This aligns with your long-term growth objectives.

✨ Advizmo AI

Your portfolio value changed because the stocks you own decreased in market price. You still own the same number of shares.

✨ Advizmo AI

This is general information, not personalized financial advice. Your situation may be different.

15 Legal Language

Keep legal copy readable. Never hide important information.

Investment Risk
Investing involves risk, including the possible loss of principal. Past performance doesn't guarantee future results.
Privacy
We never sell your data. Your financial information is encrypted and never shared with third parties for marketing purposes.
AI Disclaimer
AI recommendations are based on general financial principles and your stated preferences. They don't constitute personalized financial advice.
